Javascript + HTML5 Canvas K-Means Demonstration

  1. Click on some points in the grid on the right (they will turn white)
  2. When you have a bunch selected, choose a number of clusters for the algorithm to find and then click Go!
  3. The Circles shown are the centers of the clusters (the number you chose) of the points you selected
  4. If the results seem to suck, click Go! again and it will recalculate
  5. Click Clear (Or don't!) and try again

Uses the following frameworks:
  • JSLINQ
  • JQuery 1.4.2
  • Raphael
